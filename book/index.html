All messages and prayers (PDF)

Messages on this site, which were receieved by Maria Divine Mercy, are contained in The Book of Truth, foretold in the Book of Daniel. Jesus told her that the messages were to be compiled and produced in a Book in volumes and that it was to be called the Book of Truth.

In about four years, three volumes of these messages, as well as the Crusade Prayers, have been produced in print and eBook format, and are available through Apple iTunes, Amazon, Kobo, Overdrive and through independent publishers and resellers throughout the world.

You are not obliged to buy the books, as the messages and prayers are made available to download for free.
