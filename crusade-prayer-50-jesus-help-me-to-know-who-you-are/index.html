Crusade of Prayer (50) Jesus help me to know who You are

Monday Apr 30, 2012 at 05:45 pm

“O dear Jesus, help me to know Who You are.
Forgive me for not speaking with You, before now. Help me to find peace in this life and be shown the Truth of Eternal Life.
Soothe my heart. Ease my worries. Give me peace.
Open my heart, now, so that You can fill my soul with Your Love. Amen.”

Your Friend
Your Beloved Jesus
