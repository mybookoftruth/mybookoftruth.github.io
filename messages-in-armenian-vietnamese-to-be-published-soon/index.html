Messages in Armenian & Vietnamese to be published soon

Thursday Mar 01, 2012 at 07:15 pm

The good news is that the number of languages in which the messages are being published is increasing all the time. The next two languages, due to be uploaded on our site shortly, are in Armenian and Vietnamese.  We have also been promised a traditional Chinese version as well.

May we take this opportunity to say thank you to all the wonderful people who have given up their time, because of the love they hold in their hearts for God the Father and his beloved Son Jesus Christ to translate these messages into so many languages around the world.  We would be grateful to find translators for Russian and other Eastern European states and the Far East.
