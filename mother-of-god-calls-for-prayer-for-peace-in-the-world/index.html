Mother of God calls for prayer for peace in the world

Tuesday Sep 02, 2014 at 12:20 pm

In a message received by Maria Divine Mercy on 24 August 2014 the Mother of God, the Mother of Salvation, calls on everyone to say this prayer every day for peace in the world and for every nation. In the message she said “I ask that you recite this Crusade Prayer, the Prayer of Peace each and every day for your nations.” She refers to  Crusade Prayer (164) Prayer of Peace for Nations.
