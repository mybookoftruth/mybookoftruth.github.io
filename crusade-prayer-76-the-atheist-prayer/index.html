Crusade Prayer (76) The Atheist Prayer

Monday Sep 10, 2012 at 06:00 pm

“Jesus, help me to accept the Love of God, as it is being shown to me. Open my eyes, my mind, my heart and my soul, so that I can be saved.

Help me to believe by filling my heart with Your Love. Then hold me and save me from the torment of doubt.  Amen.”
